Peng Wu (武鹏)

NLP Researcher & Engineer

Institute of Infocommon Research

I am a NLP research engineer at the Institute of Infocommon Research. My main area of interest is Question Answering including Knowledge Based Question Answering (KBQA) and Frequency Answered Question (FAQ) which based QA pairs. I'm also interested in Dialogue especially in Dialogue Management in Task-origented Dialogue.

Previously, I received my M.Sc degree at Nanjing University Natural Language Process Group advised by Shujian Huang and Jianbing Zhang.
